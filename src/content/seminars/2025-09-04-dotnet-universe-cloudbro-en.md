## Presentation Overview

At .NET Universe CloudBro Edition, we share a cloud engineer's career journey and hands-on DevOps experiences.

## Key Topics

### Cloud Engineer Career Journey

An introduction to the growth journey starting from building CI/CD environments and gaining experience across various projects including MSA, Kubernetes, and Docker.

- Experience building CI/CD pipelines
- Participation in MSA migration projects
- Operating Kubernetes and Docker-based infrastructure

### Real-World Project Experience

- Building Kubernetes-based data platforms
- Designing and implementing authentication and authorization systems
- Know-how from operating GPU servers and storage
- Experience restructuring Kubeflow

### Lessons and Insights

Sharing knowledge and lessons gained through diverse real-world experiences. Also covers career strategies for growing as a cloud engineer.

## Target Audience

- Developers considering a career in cloud engineering
- Engineers interested in DevOps and Kubernetes
- Those looking to gain experience operating data platforms
