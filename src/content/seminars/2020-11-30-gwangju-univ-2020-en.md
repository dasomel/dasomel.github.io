A special lecture on Java-based software development and system management at Gwangju University, aimed at understanding team development and operational environments using Java in practice.

**Period**: November 30 - December 4, 2020

- Understanding version control systems
- Git-based collaboration methods
- Branch strategies

### Build and Deployment Management
- Build automation tools
- CI/CD pipeline configuration
- Understanding deployment processes

### Understanding Operational Environments
- Distinguishing development/staging/production environments
- Monitoring and logging
