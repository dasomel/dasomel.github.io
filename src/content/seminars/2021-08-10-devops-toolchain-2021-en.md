## Presentation Overview

A SW engineering technical seminar organized by NIPA (National IT Industry Promotion Agency), covering the utilization of various open source DevOps tools and the latest trends in Legacy and Cloud development environments.

**Format**: Online real-time streaming and Q&A

## Seminar Introduction

A monthly technical seminar inviting domestic and international SW experts to share the latest technology trends related to SW development and strengthen the capabilities of developers and managers.

## Key Topics

### DevOps Overview
- DevOps culture and principles
- Understanding CI/CD pipelines

### DevOps in Legacy Environment
- Tool utilization in existing environments
- Gradual transition strategy

### DevOps in Cloud Environment
- Cloud native tool chains
- Container-based CI/CD

### Open Source Tools
- Jenkins, GitLab CI
- Docker, Kubernetes
- ArgoCD, Tekton
