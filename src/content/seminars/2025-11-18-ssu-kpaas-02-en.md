## Presentation Overview

A special lecture is given as an external K-PaaS expert in the Software Special Lecture 2 course at Soongsil University. The session covers an understanding of K-PaaS development environments and deployment, followed by Q&A.

## Key Topics

### K-PaaS Development Environment Setup
- Local development environment configuration
- Introduction to K-PaaS development tools
- Understanding the development workflow

### K-PaaS Deployment Strategy
- Application deployment process
- Deployment automation methods
- Production environment management

### Hands-on and Q&A
- Development environment demonstration
- Student Q&A session

## Target Audience

- Students enrolled in Software Special Lecture 2 at Soongsil University
- Students interested in K-PaaS development environments
- Developers looking to learn cloud platform deployment
